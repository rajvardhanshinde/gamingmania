7th GAMING PLATFORM

7th gaming is an online platform that brings to you various multiplayer games.

7th gaming is an online platform that brings to you various multiplayer games. We are the first ones that provide top 7 famous games on a single site. These games include Call Of Duty, Clash Of Clans, BattleGround Mobile India, Stumble Guys, Valorant, PUBG NewState, Mini Milatia and FreeFire. We aim to create a hybrid gaming environment where various squads will first register and play on our online platform. Once they start playing and winning the matches, they would gain points and credits. On the basis of these credits, after achieiving the required number of points, top teams will be selected and called up for an offline tournament at our head office. Online registration and playing is free of cost but offline tournament will have some negligible entry fees. The teams that would win at the offline tournament will receive various swags and hampers. The prize pool will be announced before few days of the offline tournament.

Teams just need to register themselves and further all details will be communicated to the team personally by our facilitators. Entire communication will be held through the team’s mails. The squad will be provided with the timing details, opponent details and the link to start the battle. Teams cannot directly apply for the offline matches. Reaching the required limit of credits is a compulsion for offline participation. So at first, the teams should play and win good on the online platform. If the teams and players have any doubt or query regarding anything, then they can contact our facilitators on the below given contact sites.
